Keynote Speaker

Featured keynote for AutoSys 2026.

Prof. Xiaomin Ouyang, Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST).

Xiaomin Ouyang is an Assistant Professor at HKUST and leads the Mobile, Intelligent, and Networked System (MINSys) Group. Her work focuses on empowering mobile and IoT devices with AI to create intelligent and practical real-world systems.

Her recent research spans on-device and embodied AI, smart health, and mobile sensing. Prior to joining HKUST, she was a postdoctoral researcher at the UCLA Networked and Embedded Systems Lab. She received her Ph.D. from The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2023.

She received the ACM MobiSys Best Paper Award and the ACM SIGBED China Outstanding Doctoral Dissertation Award, and was recognized as one of the EECS Rising Stars in 2023.
