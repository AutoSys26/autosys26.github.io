Keynote Speaker

Dr. Xiaomin Ouyang, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology

Assistant Professor, Department of Computer Science and Engineering, HKUST

Dr. Xiaomin Ouyang leads the Mobile, Intelligent, and Networked System (MINSys) Group at HKUST. Her research focuses on mobile, intelligent, and networked systems, with particular interests in AI-enabled mobile and IoT systems.
